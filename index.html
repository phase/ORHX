What is ORHX?

ORHX will be the first student led hackathon in the El Dorado County/Folsom area. Our goal is simple. Bring computer innovation, learning, and fun to students in a welcoming environment.


What better environment than the Folsom Intel Campus? Where events such as LANFest thrived under the participation of young adults in our area. Intel will be providing a main coding area, sleeping quarters, and mentorship.


Participate in your first project, bring an idea to life, have a great time. ORHX is less of a stressful competition for startup funding, and more about starting your future in computer science.


Plus we'll have VR.

LEARN.

Hackathons are all about learning as much as you can in 24 hours. Create a team. Think of an idea. Build a project. You'll have plenty of fun getting it done.

LOCATION.

ORHX is going to be held at the Folsom Intel Campus, the perfect location for a Central California Hackathon.

FUN.

ORHX isn't a hackathon competing for startup funding, our goal is fun. Come to ORHX to learn and have a great time experiencing Silicon Valley culture.

FAQ

Do I need any coding experience?

ORHX is based around learning. No coding experience is necessary to show up, but you'll have experience when you leave.

What do I need to bring?

ORHX is a 24 hour coding event, bring a sleeping bag, any toiletries you might need, and a laptop/desktop. If you do not have a computer to bring, we will provide one.

Will I be allowed to sleep?

Of course! Not everybody is built to handle 24 hours of coding. Special soundproofed rooms will be available for as much rest as you need.

What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is a 24 hour coding event, where particpants assemble teams and code to solve a problem or build a project. ORHX will have up to 4 people per team.

Will I need to pay to participate?

ORHX will be completely free for anybody who shows up. Food, entry, and mentorship will come at no cost to you.

What will I do there?

You'll have over 24 hours to do whatever you want! You can work on projects, meet new people, and enjoy the hackathon atmosphere.
